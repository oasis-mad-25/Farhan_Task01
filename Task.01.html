What is Macaroni Pasta?

Macaroni Pasta is a Indian style masala pasta made using the elbow macaroni pasta variety.It is a delicious alternate to while sauce or any rich pasta dishes.Macaroni Pasta combines the Italian flavors and is packed with Indian masala flavors hence a fusion dish.

Macaorni is a variety of pasta used for many dishes.However you can substitute this recipe with other pasta varieties like penne, fussili, rigatoni etc. It is used to make pasta, kheer, salads etc.

Macaroni is a dry pasta variety which is more like a curved tube.The curved tube variety is elbow macaroni pasta which I have used here and the other variety is a narrow tube.But the most commen variety available here is the elbow macaroni.

I always love to include Indian flavors in pasta and this Indian style Macaroni Pasta is one of my family favorites. It is a basic onion tomato based curry made and blended with cooked pasta. This recipe is medium spicy hence suitable for kids too.

I have not used cheese or cream to make it creamy and rich but if you wish you can add it, kindly check my variations section for it. I have used just our basic Indian spices like red chili powder and garam masala powder along with mixed herbs.

I used my dutch oven to make this pasta. You can use any heavy bottom pan to make this. This is a simple macaroni pasta recipe however you can make macaroni pasta Italian style too. Macaroni pasta sauce can be prepared well ahead then mixed with pasta at the time of serving, this way it helps in making in bulk for get together and parties.

Macaroni Pasta Ingredients

  • Spices – Indian spice powders like red chilli powder, garam masala powders are used.

    How to make Macaroni Pasta step by Step

    1. Brings 4 cups wateer to boil in a large pot.Get ready with all other ingredients.
    2. When water starts to boil add 1 teaspoon oil along with salt.LEt it boil.
    3. Add 1 heaped cup macaroni pasta variety.
    4. Give a quick mix.
    5. Cook until for tender.It may take 5-6 mins depending upon the pasta variety and size. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta cooked water for later use.
    6. Drain water and Set aside.
    7. Rinse it with cold water to prevent further cooking.
    8. Heat oil in a pan – add 1 teaspoon garlic, 1/2 teaspoon ginger chopped finely. Saute for a minute until golden.
    9. Then add 1/2 cup onion finely chopped along with salt.
    10. Saute till transparent and golden.
    11. Add 1 cup tomatoes(2 medium sized), 1/4 cup capsicum, 2 tablespoon carrots finely chopped.
    12. Saute till tomatoes turn mushy and raw smell leaves.
    13. Cook covered for few mins or until the veggies turn slightly soft.
    14. Add 1 teaspoon red chilli powder, 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder and 1 teaspoon mixed herbs.
    15. Give a quick saute.
    16. Add 1 tablespoon tomato sauce along with 1/2 cup pasta cooked water.
    17. Give a quick mix and let the sauce boil for a minute.
    18. Finally add cooked pasta.
    19. Toss well until the masalas are well coated.
    20. Add 1 teaspoon chopped coriander leaves. The pasta should not be dry.
    21. Give a quick mix and switch off.
      22. Hot Macaroni Pasta ready!

    Expret Tips

    • Always reserve pasta cooked water and add it to the curry before adding pasta. This helps in thickening the sauce and also avoids pasta from drying.
    • If the pasta is not cooked soft then it will not absorb the masalas fully so make sure to cook pasta perfectly.
    • Make sure the spices are more as it will perfect after adding and mixing the pasta.
    • You can add 2 tablespoon cheese for garnish. You can either use Parmesan cheese or cheddar cheese.
    • Add 3 tablespoon mayonnaise to the sauce to make it more creamy.

    Serving & Storage

    Serve it as such hot or serve it along with toast of bread.I would recommend to consume it fresh.In case you want to store.make the sauce separately store in fridge then mix it with pasta while serving.
